What it does

OpenAd uses Apple's Screen Time (Family Controls) to gate the apps you choose. Opening a gated app prompts you to watch a rewarded ad to unlock it for a set time — 5 minutes for one ad, 15 minutes for three. The choice to unlock is always yours, and you can close the ad at any point.

Privacy in short

There's no account and no sign-in, and your Screen Time and app-selection data stay on your device — we never receive them. Ads are served by Google AdMob, which collects limited device data to show them. We don't sell your data. Full Privacy Policy.
