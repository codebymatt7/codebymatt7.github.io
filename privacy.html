Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 24, 2026  ·  Last updated: June 24, 2026

OpenAd is built to help you use your phone more intentionally, so privacy is central to how it works. The app has no account and no server of ours — your Screen Time and app-selection data stay on your device. The app shows rewarded ads through third-party advertising partners, which collect limited device data to serve them (details below).

This Privacy Policy explains what information the OpenAd app (the "App", "we", "us") handles and the choices you have. The App is operated by Seven Agent Studios ("the developer"). By using the App you agree to this Policy and to our Terms of Service.

1. Summary

  • No account required. The App works without registration or a login. We may offer optional account features in the future (see §6).
  • Your data stays on your device. Your gated-app choices, usage activity, and stats are stored only on your phone.
  • Screen Time data is never transmitted to us or to advertisers.
  • Ads are served by third-party ad partners, which collect limited device data to show rewarded ads.
  • We don't sell your personal data.

2. Information we handle

a. Screen Time & app-selection data (on-device only)

The App uses Apple's Family Controls, Managed Settings, and Device Activity frameworks. The apps, categories, and websites you choose to gate — and the on-device activity that triggers a shield — are represented by opaque tokens Apple keeps on your device. This is stored only in a private, on-device App Group container. It is never sent to our servers (we have none) and is never shared with advertisers or any third party, consistent with Apple's App Store Review Guideline 5.1.2.

b. Usage stats (on-device only)

The App keeps simple counters — such as the number of unlocks and ads watched — to show you on the dashboard. These never leave your device and are deleted when you delete the App.

c. Advertising data

Rewarded ads are delivered by third-party advertising partners. OpenAd currently uses Liftoff Monetize (Vungle), and we may add or change advertising partners (such as other rewarded-ad networks) over time. To serve and measure ads, an ad partner's SDK may collect limited information such as device identifiers, IP address, coarse location, and ad-interaction data, as described in that partner's privacy policy. We never provide ad partners with your Screen Time or app-selection data.

Depending on your choice in Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt, these ads may be personalized or non-personalized. You can change this at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking. Ads are also attributed using Apple's privacy-preserving SKAdNetwork.

d. Diagnostics & analytics

We may use aggregate, privacy-respecting analytics and crash-diagnostics tools to understand how the App is used and to fix bugs. These tools do not receive your Screen Time or app-selection data, and we use them only to improve the App.

4. Data retention & deletion

Because all app data lives only on your device, deleting the App permanently removes it. There is no account or server-side copy to request deletion of. If we later add optional accounts, we will provide a way to delete any account data.

5. Children's privacy

The App is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Future features & accounts

OpenAd may evolve over time. We may, in the future, introduce optional features such as accounts or syncing your stats across devices, additional advertising or analytics partners, or other capabilities. If and when we do, we will update this Policy to describe how any new data is handled before those features collect it.

7. Your rights

Depending on where you live (e.g. the EEA/UK under GDPR, or California under CCPA/CPRA), you may have rights regarding personal data. Because we hold no personal data about you on any server, most concerns are addressed simply by the on-device, no-account design. For questions, contact us below.

8. Changes to this Policy and our Terms

We may update this Privacy Policy and our Terms of Service at any time, including as the App's features, advertising partners, or capabilities change. When we make material changes we will update the "Last updated" date above. Your continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy and Terms.
