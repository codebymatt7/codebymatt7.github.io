Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 9, 2026  ·  Last updated: June 9, 2026

OpenAd is built to help you use your phone more intentionally, so privacy is central to how it works. The app has no account, no sign-in, and no server of ours — your Screen Time and app-selection data stay on your device. The app does show rewarded ads through Google AdMob, which collects limited device data to serve them (details below).

This Privacy Policy explains what information the OpenAd app (the "App", "we", "us") handles and the choices you have. The App is operated by Matt Neves.

1. Summary

  • No account, no login. We don't ask you to register and we don't create a profile for you.
  • Your data stays on your device. Your gated-app choices, usage activity, and stats are stored only on your phone.
  • Screen Time data is never transmitted to us or to advertisers.
  • Ads are served by Google AdMob, which collects limited device data to show non-personalized rewarded ads.
  • We don't sell your data.

2. Information we handle

a. Screen Time & app-selection data (on-device only)

The App uses Apple's Family Controls, Managed Settings, and Device Activity frameworks. The apps, categories, and websites you choose to gate — and the on-device activity that triggers a shield — are represented by opaque tokens Apple keeps on your device. This is stored only in a private, on-device App Group container. It is never sent to our servers (we have none) and is never shared with advertisers or any third party, consistent with Apple's App Store Review Guideline 5.1.2.

b. Usage stats (on-device only)

The App keeps simple counters — such as the number of unlocks, ads watched, and an estimate of time saved — to show you on the dashboard. These never leave your device and are deleted when you delete the App.

c. Advertising data

Rewarded ads are delivered by Google AdMob. To serve and measure non-personalized ads, Google's SDK may collect limited information such as device identifiers, IP address, and ad-interaction data, as described in Google's policies. We do not provide Google with your Screen Time or app-selection data.

4. Data retention & deletion

Because all app data lives only on your device, deleting the App permanently removes it. There is no account or server-side copy to request deletion of.

5. Children's privacy

The App is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Your rights

Depending on where you live (e.g. the EEA/UK under GDPR, or California under CCPA/CPRA), you may have rights regarding personal data. Because we hold no personal data about you on any server, most concerns are addressed simply by the on-device, no-account design. For questions, contact us below.

7. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time; material changes will be reflected by a new "Last updated" date.
